Privacy Policy

Effective March 2026  ·  hellotabeeb.pk

HelloTabeeb ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the HelloTabeeb mobile application ("App") within Pakistan.

By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1 Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Account password (stored in encrypted form)

Lab Test Booking Information

  • Age
  • Chief complaint / symptoms (when required for tests with doctor review)
  • Uploaded prescription documents or medical reports (optional)

Authentication Data

When you sign in using Google Sign-In, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from Google, subject to Google's own Privacy Policy.

Device & Usage Data

  • Device model and operating system
  • IP address
  • App usage information
  • Crash logs and diagnostics

Location Data

Location is collected only with your explicit consent to provide location-based services (e.g., nearby labs). You may deny or revoke this permission at any time via your device settings.

2 How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Enable user account creation, authentication, and management
  • Process and manage lab test bookings
  • Pre-fill booking forms for a smoother experience
  • Send appointment confirmations and notifications
  • Enable prescription or report uploads for lab bookings
  • Improve app functionality, performance, and user experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations of Pakistan
Note: Doctor appointment booking is a planned future feature. When available, it will be covered under an updated version of this Privacy Policy.

We do not sell or rent your personal or health-related information to any third party.

3 Data Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Lab partners (e.g., Chughtai Labs) to process your test bookings
  • Firebase (Google) — our cloud backend for authentication, database, and file storage
  • Payment service providers, if online payment is introduced in the future
  • Regulatory or legal authorities in Pakistan when required by applicable law

All third-party service providers are required to handle your information securely and are not permitted to use it for their own marketing purposes.

4 Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Firebase secure cloud infrastructure
  • Email verification required upon account creation
  • Encrypted password storage (managed by Firebase Authentication)

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information.

5 Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required under applicable Pakistani laws. You may request account deletion by contacting us at info@hellotabeeb.com. Upon request, we will delete your account and associated personal data, subject to any legal obligations to retain certain records.

6 Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental or guardian consent. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent for optional data collection (e.g., location, profile photo)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@hellotabeeb.com.

8 Third-Party Links & Services

The App may integrate with third-party services (e.g., Google Sign-In, Lab partner portals). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

9 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services or applicable laws. The updated version will be posted within the App and on our website. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
